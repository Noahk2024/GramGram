Bingo

A Timeless Classic.

A game in which players mark off numbers on cards as the numbers are drawn randomly by a caller, the winner being the first person to mark off five numbers in a row or another required pattern.

  • Game: Bingo

Go Fish

Carpe Diem!

A card game in which each player in turn asks an opponent for a particular card and is told to “go fish” from the undealt deck if denied.

  • Game: Go Fish

Chess

Gens una sumus

A board game of strategic skill for two players, played on a checkered board. Each player begins the game with sixteen pieces that are moved and used to capture opposing pieces according to precise rules. The object is to put the opponent's king under a direct attack from which escape is impossible ( checkmate ).

  • Game: Chess

Yahtzee

Roll the Dice!

A probability game where players amass scores depending on the values of the five dice they roll.

  • Game: Yahtzee

Dominoes

¡No Mas!

Dominoes is a family of tile-based games played with gaming pieces, commonly known as dominoes. Each domino is a rectangular tile, usually with a line dividing its face into two square ends. Each end is marked with a number of spots (also called pips or dots) or is blank. The backs of the tiles in a set are indistinguishable, either blank or having some common design.

  • Game: Dominoes

Checkers

Hop Hop Hop

Checkers, also known as draughts, is a group of strategy board games for two players which involve diagonal moves of uniform game pieces and mandatory captures by jumping over opponent pieces.

  • Game: Checkers
